ApnaWalk - Smart AI Pedometer & Step Counter

ApnaWalk is a professional fitness application designed for users who want to track their daily physical activity with precision. Using advanced device sensors and GPS tracking, we provide accurate step counts, calorie burn estimates, and route mapping.

Key Features

  • Real-time Step Counter: Accurate background tracking.
  • AI Fitness Coach: Personal insights powered by Gemini AI in Hinglish.
  • GPS Route Mapping: Visualize your walks on interactive dark-mode maps.
  • Hydration Tracker: Smart reminders based on your activity and local weather.
  • Social Hub: Join walking groups and compete in global challenges.
  • Privacy First: Your health data stays on your device.
